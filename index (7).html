03 — Location Tracker
Certification to Draw Request
Track every service address from Identified through TDED certification — the exact workflow required for BEAD draw request submissions. The tracker adapts its stages and field labels for BEAD, FTTH, HFC, 5G, and OSP project types. A draw request readiness alert fires automatically when all locations reach certified status. For non-BEAD projects, serves as a serviceable address database and construction status log per address.

Site Images into Project Intelligence
Upload a job site photograph from any mobile browser on the job site. The platform's vision AI analyzes the image within seconds and returns a structured assessment: construction progress indicators, safety hazards, quality issues with materials or workmanship, equipment conditions, and specific corrective actions. Findings are automatically written to the project risk register and field activity log — no manual data entry required.
